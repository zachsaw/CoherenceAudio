The USB Clock Drift Problem

Standard software forces all mics into one mixed track. If you try to record multiple USB mics independently, their internal hardware clocks drift out of sync over time, ruining your audio phase.

The Coherence Way: Plug in standard USB microphones. Coherence Audio records them raw, and the Pro Edition automatically corrects hardware clock drift to the millisecond.
